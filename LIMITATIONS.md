# Limitations

- No experimental O2 isotherm for Al-Fum was identified.
- O2 adsorption is approximated using N2-derived surrogate isotherms.
- 273 K and 313 K CH4/N2 selectivities should be interpreted as sensitivity analyses due to low-loading N2 interpolation instability.
- The 298 K result is considered the primary validated result.
- Humidity (H2O) is not yet included and is expected to have a larger impact on performance than O2.
