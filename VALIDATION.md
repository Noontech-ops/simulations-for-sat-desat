
# Validation

![Validation Figure](figures/validation_literature_vs_simulation.png)

## Literature

- 273 K selectivity ≈ 17.2
- 298 K selectivity ≈ 11.7

## Simulation

- 273 K = 36.69
- 298 K = 10.74
- 313 K = 62.85

## Conclusion

The 298 K CH4/N2 selectivity prediction (10.74) closely matches the literature value (~11.7), supporting model validity near ambient conditions. Results at 273 K and 313 K should be interpreted as sensitivity analyses due to instability associated with low-loading N2 interpolation.
